About

I'm Jamie Hoyle, the Conservative candidate for Ramsbottom, Shuttleworth and Holcombe in the May 2018 local elections.

Having grown up in the heart of Ramsbottom, I've seen our community grow and change as the years go by. I'm standing for councillor because I believe I can help shape and deliver positive change for our local area.

I started my education at Emmanuel Holcombe, before continuing onwards to BRGS and their sixth form. After my A-Levels, I took a gap year to start a software consultancy based in Summerseat. I moved into a new role as an executive at a web archiving company in Manchester early last year.

As someone who went down a non-traditional career path, education is a key priority of mine. I want to be a strong voice on Bury Council for young people, and I want to use my experiences to help others like me across the ward. I also want to throw myself into the everyday life of a local councillor, working with everyone from pensioners to young families to make life in our town even better.

In the coming weeks, I'll begin detailing my policy positions and hosting events so that people can get to know me and ask me questions face-to-face. Until then, you can follow the start of my campaign using the links on the left. I can't wait to meet even more of my neighbours on the campaign trail!
